Movie Review

Ocean's 11

Ocean's 11 is about a guy who gathers a group of crimminals to rob the biggest chain of casino's in Las Vegas. There are three casinos linked together with the same banking system. They all go to the undeground vault. The criminals make a insane replica of the vault and practice a lot. They bond and find crazy ways to make thier hiest even better. It is all preperation for the big day where it gets so crazy that at some parts it hard to keep track of whats happening. The plan works out so perfect and its crazy these people arnt that smart.

My Review

Ocean's 11 is one of my favorite movies. I loved all the actors in it, some of them are George Cloony and Matt Damon. It was a well thought out movie which made watching it over and over possible. It is weird though how when the prospective of the movie changes, you change sides. Normally you wouldn't want to root for the robbers but in this movie you do end up cheering for them. It could be just the way they made the movie or the lovable cast but I love watching this movie.
